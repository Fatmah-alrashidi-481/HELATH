What is nutrition

Nutrition is a science that explains the relationship of food to the activities of living things. These include eating food, flushing out waste, releasing energy from the body, and creating processes. Food and drink provide the person with energy for all the vital functions of the body, so its temperature is fixed at 37 degrees Celsius, suitable for the vital functions of the body, whether during waking or during sleep
