MY DOCTOR

What is the importance of the doctor in our lives

The medical profession is one of the noblest and most noble professions. This human profession is used by those who carry mercy in their hearts, wandering like angels with their white robe, noble smile, and good morals, relieving the sick of their pain, sorrows, and diseases, and growers to smile on their faces, and watch over their comfort. Before reassuring those patients and making sure of their safety, so they consider half of a person’s healing with the kindness of their souls which are like balsam, their great humanity, the card of their hearts.

What is the role of the family doctor

The family doctor is the first stage for us for every medical problem. And what we go to immediately after the medical problem arises: disease, pain, inflammation, infections or something else.
